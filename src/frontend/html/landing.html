Bridging Heritage, Health & Modern Science

Digital Sangoma is an AI-powered Cultural-Ethnobotany and Ancestral Knowledge System that preserves indigenous wisdom while connecting it to modern scientific understanding.

Powerful Cultural Preservation Tools

Our platform combines ancestral wisdom with modern technology to create a living repository of indigenous knowledge.

AI Plant Identification

Use your phone's camera to identify indigenous medicinal plants and learn about their traditional uses and scientific properties.

Ancestral Knowledge Graph

Explore interconnected wisdom from oral traditions, rituals, and healing practices preserved through machine learning.

Digital Ritual Calendar

Sync with lunar cycles, seasonal changes, and traditional holidays to stay connected to cultural practices.

Elder-Youth Knowledge Bridge

Connect with cultural knowledge keepers to learn directly from elders and preserve intergenerational wisdom.
