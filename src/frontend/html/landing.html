Bridging Heritage, Health & Modern Science

Imbewu is an AI-powered Cultural-Ethnobotany and Ancestral Knowledge System that preserves indigenous wisdom while connecting it to modern scientific understanding. We're planting seeds of knowledge that grow into healing for communities.

The Crisis Imbewu Solves

80% of South Africans use traditional medicine, but knowledge erosion is making it dangerously unreliable.

Knowledge Crisis

Elders are passing away without transferring precise plant knowledge, leading to 2,000+ annual poisonings from misidentified plants.

Safety Crisis

Dangerous interactions between traditional remedies and modern medicines cause hospitalizations and preventable deaths.

Access Crisis

14 million South Africans can't afford modern healthcare while traditional healers are overwhelmed and scattered.

Powerful Cultural Preservation Tools

Our platform combines ancestral wisdom with modern technology to create a living repository of indigenous knowledge.

AI Plant Identification

Use computer vision to identify indigenous medicinal plants with traditional uses and modern pharmacological data. Discover both cultural significance and scientific properties.

Ancestral Knowledge Graph

ML-built knowledge graph from oral stories, cultural practices, and healing traditions. Preserves endangered languages & wisdom by digitizing elders' teachings.

Cultural Seasons Calendar

Syncs with lunar cycles, seasonal changes and traditional observances. Recommends optimal planting times and cultural celebration periods.

Intergenerational Knowledge Bridge

ML-based matching connects youth seeking wisdom with local elders and knowledge keepers. Preserves intergenerational wisdom digitally.
